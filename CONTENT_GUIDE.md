## Creating Content: Best Practices

### Research Sources

- **Official Documentation**
  - Manufacturer service manuals
  - Industry standards (IMI, City & Guilds)
  - British Standards (BS)

- **Professional References**
  - Bosch automotive manuals
  - Denso technical guides
  - Engineering textbooks

- **Online Resources**
  - YouTube technical channels
  - Technical blogs
  - Industry publications

### Content Structure

Each pair should follow this pattern:

**Card 1 (Name/Concept):**
- Title: Component/System name
- Content: What it is
- 1-2 emojis

**Card 2 (Description/Function):**
- Title: Function/Operation
- Content: How it works, why it matters
- 2-3 bullet points (using \n)
